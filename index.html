About Hannah

I'm a passionate Toronto-based web developer with a diverse skillset that includes a mix of front-end development and graphic design abilities. I'm always learning and expanding on my web knowledge, and having fun while doing so! I love working together with people to create clean and beautiful code.

Away from my keyboard I enjoy exploring the city's greenspaces, playing videogames, making small craft projects, and theatre.

I believe that anything is possible through the combined powers of the internet and determination (and a good night's sleep). I want to create beautiful digital experiences everyone can share and enjoy.
